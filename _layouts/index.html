About us

Who We Are

St. Vladimir Memorial Church is a Russian Orthodox Church in the jurisdiction of Russian Orthodox Church Outside of Russia (ROCOR). Our magnificent church was build in commemoration of 1000 year anniversary of the Baptizm of Rus' by Prince Vladimir.

Church Life

Every Sunday we serve Divine Liturgy.

Sunday school - pupils learn Russian, Theology, Classic Literatire.

Sisterhood makes luncheons every Sunday, following the Divine Liturgy.

Special feasts during the year:

  • St. Vladimir's Day - the last Sunday of July. Festival at our church includes Praying, Blessing of Water, Cultural Music, Russian Food, Market and Book Store
  • Maslenitsa - First Sunday of March. Last feast before Great Lent. Fish, Caviar, Raffles, Music.
